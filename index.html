Intro: Why I Created Feral Goth

As a real goth, and an elder of the global goth community, I am learning that the Goth Culture's non-consumerist online presence needs to be revived.

There is no standing tome to goth culture the way their used to be.

And as an elder in the community, I find it my obligation and duty to represent the culture and teach the next generations what goth culture really means at its core.  

If you cannot face death, you are not really living your life. 

So many people who are conditioned to think normally...do not allow themselves to challenge the way they think on automatic, as if they are not really choosing anything. As if all the information provided to them is not worthy of their critical thinking capabilities.

As a goth, it is my first responsibility to question everything, and make sure I'm not lying to myself, about anything.

"Think for yourself. Question authority."

Often times, because this western society is living in a state of apocalypse, not lying to myself can mean that I am experiencing something like depression, because let's be real, shit in this life is fuckin sad.   

On this website, you will find dry humor and sardonic commentary about living in modern Western culture, plus a good dose of things that bring me inspiration. All within a goth context,

as I will explain...

The goth perspective: So many people are living a pathetic existence because they are lost in lies and living as zombies.
